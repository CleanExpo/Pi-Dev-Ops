## THE COMPLIANCE BURDEN

### You're spending too much time managing compliance. And it's still not enough.

Running a trade business means managing operations, staff, subcontractors, and clients — all while staying across a compliance surface that expands every year. Most businesses don't have a system. They have a folder, a spreadsheet, and the memory of whoever last touched it.

That's not a compliance program. That's a liability waiting to be found.

**The three problems that cost trade businesses the most:**

---

**1. Audit preparation is a crisis every time.**
When a regulatory body or client requests documentation, it shouldn't require three days and an emergency all-hands to gather evidence you should already have. Audit readiness isn't a project — it's a standard you either maintain or scramble to fake.

**2. Regulatory changes arrive without warning.**
WHS legislation updates. Building codes change. Insurance requirements shift. Most businesses find out they're non-compliant when someone raises it — not before. By then, the gap is already a liability.

**3. Documentation falls through the gaps.**
Subcontractor certifications expire. Safety plans go unsigned. Induction records don't get filed. Each gap is small. Collectively, they constitute a compliance program that cannot be defended.

## COMPLIANCE STANDARDS

### Aligned to the frameworks that matter in ANZ.

CARSI is configured to support compliance delivery across the regulatory frameworks that apply to trade businesses operating in Australia and New Zealand, including:

- **Work Health and Safety (WHS) Act 2011** — Safe Work Australia national framework, with state and territory variations
- **ISO 9001:2015** — Quality management system requirements for businesses holding or pursuing certification
- **Building Code of Australia (NCC)** — Documentation and compliance obligations for construction and trade work
- **Australian Standard AS/NZS 4801** — Occupational health and safety management systems
- **ISO 45001:2018** — International OHS management standard, increasingly required for major contracts
- **Workers' Compensation requirements** — State-specific obligations for subcontractor and employee coverage
- **Contractor and Subcontractor Management** — Prequalification and compliance documentation requirements for principal contractors
- **Environmental compliance** — Applicable obligations for restoration, remediation, and construction work

CARSI's framework library is maintained and updated as regulatory requirements change, so your compliance workflows remain aligned without manual review.
