# DR — The Restoration Business That Runs Without You

Duration: 60s
Channel: LinkedIn
Brand: dr

## Scene 1 — The Overwhelmed Owner (10s)
Visual: Restoration business owner at their desk — three monitors, phones ringing, sticky notes everywhere, spreadsheet open, WhatsApp notifications stacking. Split-screen shows: crew texting "which job today?", a client calling unanswered, an invoice spreadsheet with red cells. Owner looks stretched.
Voiceover: "You built this business. But somewhere along the way, it started running you."
On-screen text: "Every restoration business owner hits this wall."
Transition: sweep

## Scene 2 — DR Dashboard Loads (12s)
Visual: Owner opens DR on laptop. Clean dashboard materialises — job pipeline in columns (Assessed / Active / Drying / Complete / Invoiced), each card showing job address, team member assigned, status, and a completion percentage. Everything in one view. No spreadsheets. No sticky notes.
Voiceover: "DR is the operating system for restoration businesses — built by people who've actually run them."
On-screen text: "One dashboard. Every job. Every team member. Every dollar."
Transition: rise

## Scene 3 — Jobs Tracked (12s)
Visual: Zoom into a single job card — drying readings auto-imported from equipment, daily photos from the site team, adjuster correspondence logged, invoice status live. Timeline bar shows job progression. Notification: "Day 3 moisture readings below threshold — ready to pack out." Owner taps "Approve" without picking up the phone.
Voiceover: "Every job updates in real time. Drying logs, photo documentation, adjuster notes — all captured automatically, no chasing your crew."
On-screen text: "Auto drying logs · Photo trail · Adjuster comms · One tap approvals"
Transition: pulse

## Scene 4 — Team Coordinated (12s)
Visual: Split-screen — left: DR dispatch board showing three technicians with assigned jobs, routes on map, start times. Right: technician's phone showing their job for the day — address, scope, checklist, and documentation camera ready. Team chat thread shows a resolved question that never reached the owner. Dispatch confirmation: "All crews deployed — no calls required."
Voiceover: "Your team knows exactly where to go, what to do, and how to document it. You stop being the bottleneck."
On-screen text: "Dispatch · Scheduling · Team accountability — without the phone calls"
Transition: sweep

## Scene 5 — Owner Takes a Day Off (14s)
Visual: Owner closes laptop on a Friday afternoon. Grabs keys — cut to: coffee with spouse, kids' sport, a run. Phone screen visible in pocket: dark. No notifications. No missed calls. Quick cut back to DR dashboard — two new jobs auto-assigned, one invoice sent, drying readings logged. System ran itself. Owner's face: relief.
Voiceover: "The day you realise your phone stayed dark all day — that's the day you know DR is working. Book a demo. Build the business that doesn't need you to run."
On-screen text: "drplatform.com — Book Your Demo"
Transition: fade
