# Synthex — Your Brand Voice, Everywhere at Once

Duration: 60s
Channel: LinkedIn
Brand: synthex

## Scene 1 — The Brand Voice Problem (8s)
Visual: Split screen — left side shows 4 different writers producing 4 wildly inconsistent emails; fonts clash, tone varies, one is formal, one is casual. Red X marks appear over each.
Voiceover: "Every piece of content your team writes sounds like a different company. Customers notice."
On-screen text: "Your brand has a voice. Is anyone using it?"
Transition: sweep

## Scene 2 — Synthex Learns Your Voice (12s)
Visual: Synthex interface open on a sleek monitor. User uploads brand guidelines, past emails, and tone samples. An animated "learning" progress arc fills — DNA helix morphs into a voice waveform. The UI shows "Brand Profile: Locked In."
Voiceover: "Synthex reads your brand guidelines, your best-performing content, your tone — and learns exactly how you sound."
On-screen text: "Brand Voice AI — trained on your content"
Transition: rise

## Scene 3 — Multi-Platform Publishing (12s)
Visual: Single piece of content drafted in Synthex. With one click, it fragments and adapts — LinkedIn post, email newsletter, Instagram caption, SMS — all populating simultaneously in their native formats. Icons for each platform light up in sequence.
Voiceover: "One brief. One click. Perfectly adapted content for every channel — on-brand, every time."
On-screen text: "LinkedIn · Email · Instagram · SMS"
Transition: sweep

## Scene 4 — Analytics Dashboard (10s)
Visual: Full-screen Synthex analytics dashboard. Line graphs climbing. Open rate tracker, engagement heatmaps, A/B test winner badges. Numbers animate upward in real time.
Voiceover: "Track what's working. Synthex ties every piece of content to performance data so you can stop guessing."
On-screen text: "Real-time performance across every channel"
Transition: pulse

## Scene 5 — The 34% Proof (10s)
Visual: Bold stat card dominates the frame — "34% average open rate increase" stamped in Synthex brand colour. Below it, a testimonial quote fades in from a recognisable B2B brand logo.
Voiceover: "Our customers see an average 34% lift in email open rates within the first 90 days. Not a projection — a result."
On-screen text: "34% average open rate increase · 90-day result"
Transition: rise

## Scene 6 — CTA (8s)
Visual: Clean Synthex homepage on screen. Cursor moves to "Start Free Trial" button and clicks. Confetti burst. URL and brand mark hold centre frame.
Voiceover: "Stop publishing noise. Start publishing your brand. Try Synthex free for 14 days."
On-screen text: "synthex.io — Start Free Trial"
Transition: fade
