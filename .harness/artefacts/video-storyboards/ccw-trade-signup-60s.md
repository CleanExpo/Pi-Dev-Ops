# CCW — Trade Pricing. Real Savings. Same Day.

Duration: 60s
Channel: Instagram/Facebook
Brand: ccw

## Scene 1 — Retail Frustration (10s)
Visual: Trade professional (plumber, builder) standing in a big-box hardware store, scanning a shelf price tag. Their expression drops — the price is obviously painful. They pull out their phone to check a quote they sent a client. The numbers don't stack up. They shake their head.
Voiceover: "You're a trade professional. So why are you paying retail prices?"
On-screen text: "Paying retail? That's eating your margin."
Transition: sweep

## Scene 2 — Discovers CCW (12s)
Visual: Same professional in their ute/van, scrolling on phone. CCW website loads — clean trade-focused homepage. "Trade Accounts" section highlights — they lean in, interested. Tagline: "Built for the trade. Priced for the trade." Logo and product categories appear — electrical, plumbing, building supplies.
Voiceover: "CCW is a trade supply partner built differently — pricing structured around your business, not retail margins."
On-screen text: "CCW — Trade Supply, Trade Pricing"
Transition: rise

## Scene 3 — Signs Up Trade Account (12s)
Visual: Screen recording-style capture of the CCW trade account signup — fast, 3 fields: Business name, ABN/trade licence, contact. "Apply" tapped. "Approved — Welcome to CCW Trade" confirmation screen appears with a green tick. The professional pumps their fist.
Voiceover: "Signing up takes two minutes. Your trade account is approved the same day."
On-screen text: "2-minute signup · Same-day approval · No lock-in contracts"
Transition: pulse

## Scene 4 — Saves 30% (14s)
Visual: Side-by-side price comparison animation — retail price on left (crossed out in red), CCW trade price on right (highlighted in green). "30% average saving" badge stamps over the comparison. Shopping cart fills with items — total at checkout is visibly lower. Savings calculator on screen shows dollar figure.
Voiceover: "Our trade members save an average of 30% compared to retail — and that adds up fast across a full job."
On-screen text: "Average 30% saving vs. retail · Your margin stays intact"
Transition: sweep

## Scene 5 — Same-Day Dispatch Arrives (12s)
Visual: CCW warehouse — order being picked and packed in fast motion. Cut to: delivery van pulling up to a job site. Driver hands over a CCW-branded box. Trade professional opens it, holds up the part they needed — job resumes immediately. Thumbs up. CCW logo + CTA card holds on screen.
Voiceover: "Order before noon, and we dispatch same day. Your job keeps moving. Your business keeps growing. That's the CCW trade advantage."
On-screen text: "Order by 12pm → Same-day dispatch" | "ccw.com.au — Open your trade account today"
