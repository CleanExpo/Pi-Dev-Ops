# CARSI — From Audit Letter to Green Light

Duration: 45s
Channel: LinkedIn
Brand: carsi

## Scene 1 — The Audit Letter (10s)
Visual: Business owner at kitchen table, morning coffee going cold. Opens an official-looking letter — "Notice of Compliance Audit" header visible. Their face shifts: colour drains, jaw tightens. They set the letter down and stare at it. Laptop opens instinctively, multiple browser tabs fly open — regulations, requirements, past filing records — chaos.
Voiceover: "An audit letter lands in your inbox and suddenly you're not sure if everything is in order. That feeling — that's what CARSI exists to eliminate."
On-screen text: "Audit Notice Received." [pause] "Do you know where you stand?"
Transition: sweep

## Scene 2 — CARSI Dashboard (12s)
Visual: CARSI platform loads — clean compliance dashboard. Left panel: obligation register, all relevant regulations listed and mapped to the business's profile. Centre: status indicators — green checkmarks, yellow caution flags, one red item (an overdue filing). Right: upcoming deadlines calendar. Everything the auditor will ask about is already here, organised, dated, evidenced.
Voiceover: "CARSI maps every compliance obligation to your business — licences, filings, certifications, renewals — and tracks the evidence in one place."
On-screen text: "Every obligation. Every deadline. Every piece of evidence — organised."
Transition: rise

## Scene 3 — Compliance Status: Green (12s)
Visual: Owner works through CARSI — clicks the one red item, uploads a document, marks it complete. The red indicator flips to green. Overall compliance score bar climbs to 100%. Dashboard header updates: "Compliance Status: Ready for Audit." Owner sits back — exhales. Exports a compliance report — polished, comprehensive, audit-ready.
Voiceover: "One by one, every gap closes. When CARSI shows green, you're not just compliant — you're audit-ready with evidence to prove it."
On-screen text: "Compliance Status: READY" | "Audit-ready report — generated in one click"
Transition: pulse

## Scene 4 — Audit Passed. Relief. (11s)
Visual: Business owner back at a desk — now in a professional setting. Video call with an auditor: auditor nods, clicks through the CARSI-generated report, types notes. Final message on screen: "Audit Complete — No Outstanding Issues." Owner closes laptop, leans back, smiles — genuine relief. CARSI logo + CTA card holds on clean background.
Voiceover: "Audit passed. No surprises. No scrambling. Just proof you were running a compliant business all along. CARSI — compliance confidence, every day."
On-screen text: "Audit Passed." | "carsi.com.au — Start your compliance review free"
Transition: fade
