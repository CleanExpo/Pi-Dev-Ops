# NRPG — Stop Fighting Adjusters Alone. Join the Movement.

Duration: 60s
Channel: LinkedIn
Brand: nrpg

## Scene 1 — The Adjuster Decision (10s)
Visual: Restoration contractor at a kitchen table late at night. Laptop open — email from adjuster visible: "Claim denied — insufficient documentation." Stack of invoices next to laptop. Contractor leans back, hands over face. Clock on wall reads 11pm.
Voiceover: "You did the work. You documented everything. And the adjuster still cut your invoice — or denied it outright."
On-screen text: "Sound familiar? You're not alone."
Transition: sweep

## Scene 2 — Discovers NRPG (10s)
Visual: Scrolling LinkedIn feed stops on an NRPG post — "Restoration contractors: you deserve to be paid for the work you do." Contractor sits up, clicks through. NRPG community page loads — hundreds of members, active discussions, real contractor names and faces. Not a vendor. A movement.
Voiceover: "NRPG — the National Restoration Professionals Guild — exists because contractors like you got tired of fighting alone."
On-screen text: "NRPG — Restoration Contractors, United."
Transition: rise

## Scene 3 — Joins the Movement (12s)
Visual: Fast montage — contractor fills out NRPG membership form (30 seconds on screen), instantly accesses member dashboard: training modules, document templates, adjuster negotiation playbooks, community forum. Feed shows other members posting wins: "Got paid in full today." "Adjuster backed down." "Best decision I made."
Voiceover: "When you join NRPG, you plug into a network of contractors who've already solved the problem you're staring at right now."
On-screen text: "Templates · Training · Community · Advocacy"
Transition: pulse

## Scene 4 — Structure → Systems → Scale (16s)
Visual: Three-panel framework animation, each panel rising into frame:
Panel 1 — "STRUCTURE": Blueprint overlay on a job site. Documentation systems, SOPs, contract templates locking into place.
Panel 2 — "SYSTEMS": Dashboard with job tracking, invoicing, evidence compilation flowing like a production line.
Panel 3 — "SCALE": Revenue graph climbing. More jobs, bigger contracts, less friction. Contractor shaking hands with a client — both smiling.
Voiceover: "Our framework is simple: Structure — build the foundation so every job is documented right. Systems — automate the work that eats your time. Scale — grow a restoration business that runs without you chasing every dollar."
On-screen text: "Structure → Systems → Scale"
Transition: rise

## Scene 5 — Independent. Paid. In Control. (12s)
Visual: Same contractor from Scene 1 — now in daylight. Confident posture. Reviewing a paid invoice on their phone. Walking out of a job site to a newer work vehicle. Community screen shows their name — "Member since 2024 · 47 claims closed in full." NRPG logo + CTA.
Voiceover: "This is what independence looks like in restoration. Join NRPG — and start getting paid what you're worth."
On-screen text: "nrpg.com.au — Join the Movement"
Transition: fade
