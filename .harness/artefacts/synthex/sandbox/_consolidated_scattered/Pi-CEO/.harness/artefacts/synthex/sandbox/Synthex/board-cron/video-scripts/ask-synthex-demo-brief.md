## The Scenario

**Dave** is a plumber based in Parramatta, NSW. He's been using Synthex for 3 months. His engagement dropped noticeably last week and he's not sure why. He opens the Synthex app during a lunch break.

This is a real scenario for Australian tradespeople — not a made-up demonstration. The story must feel like something Dave's mate who uses Synthex would share with him.

## Production Notes

### Screen Recording Specs

- Device: Mobile (iPhone 15 / Pixel 8 frame) — AU audience uses mobile-first
- Recording: Real Synthex staging environment with seeded "Dave" data
- Seed data required: 192 posts, realistic engagement variance, Health Score 68, 4 posts in 7–9am slots last week
- Answer: Hard-coded in staging for consistent playback — not live generation
- Source chevron: Must be visibly interactive (tapped open during recording)
- Calendar update: Must animate visibly when Dave taps "Apply"

### Steve (ElevenLabs) Voice Direction

- Tone: Conversational, unhurried — like a knowledgeable mate, not an announcer
- Pace: Slightly slower than default ElevenLabs pacing — 90% speed
- Emotion: Grounded confidence, mild warmth — no hype, no exclamation energy
- Key lines to emphasise (natural stress, not dramatic):
  - "Not a generic tip. **His data.** His posts. His audience."
  - "That answer took **2.4 seconds.**"
  - "No other platform has that data. **Only Synthex does.**"

### Captions

- Auto-generate via CapCut or Descript
- Review for accuracy before export
- Style: White bold, lower third position, no background box

### Music

- Underscore: Subtle lo-fi instrumental, fades under voiceover
- CTA moment: Music rises to ~40% volume for final 5 seconds
- License: Epidemic Sound or Artlist (existing Synthex licence)

### Thumbnail (Short)

- Frame: Synthex answer on screen mid-render, typing indicator visible
- Text overlay: **"Asked Synthex why... 😳"** (bold, sans-serif)
- DO NOT use stock photos of phones or people — use real screen recording frame
