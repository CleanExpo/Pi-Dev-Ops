## CONTENT REPURPOSING PIPELINE

### Long-form → Shorts Workflow

```
1. Record/edit long-form video (10–15 min)
2. Identify 2–3 "clip moments": hooks, stats, demonstrations under 60s
3. Export clips at 9:16 (vertical) — crop from 16:9 if needed
4. Add captions (YouTube auto-captions → review + edit)
5. Add text overlay: bold hook on first frame
6. Upload as YouTube Shorts (title must include # or "Shorts" for algorithm)
7. Schedule: Mon long-form, Wed + Fri Shorts
```

### Long-form → Social Posts (via Synthex)

- Generate 3 LinkedIn posts from video transcript
- Generate 5 Twitter/X posts (key stats + insights)
- Generate 2 Instagram carousels (step-by-step format)
- Schedule via Synthex auto-calendar

### Long-form → Blog Post

- Export transcript → clean with AI → publish at synthex.social/blog
- Add VideoObject JSON-LD schema (per SYN-542)
- Internal links to pricing and relevant features

## VELOCITY TACTICS FOR FAST SUBSCRIBER GROWTH

1. **Shorts strategy is the fastest path to 1,000 subs.** Algorithm pushes Shorts to non-subscribers. 2–3 per week in Phase 1 is non-negotiable.
2. **Use existing Synthex clients as case studies.** Real numbers beat hypotheticals. Ask 2–3 clients for permission to share their dashboard growth on camera.
3. **Comment on larger AU/NZ SMB channels** (Ravi Abuvala, Dan Norris, Lachlan Kirkwood) — builds cross-audience awareness.
4. **Post the first video on LinkedIn.** Phill's personal LinkedIn audience bootstraps the channel's first 50–100 subscribers instantly.
5. **Submit to AU/NZ business subreddits** (r/AusFinance, r/AustralianBusiness, r/smallbusiness_au) — high-intent audience, minimal competition.
