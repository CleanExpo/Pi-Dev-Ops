## Approval Gates

An asset cannot move to client-ready unless:

- Product facts are accurate.
- CTA matches the landing page.
- Claims are evidence-linked.
- No fake customer story or testimonial is used.
- Product screenshots are approved.
- Audio/music has licence evidence.
- Founder voice or likeness has consent evidence if used.
- Captions are readable on mobile.
- Format variants export cleanly.

## Launch-Ready Definition

The campaign is launch-ready when:

- Landing page trust check passes.
- At least one LinkedIn post is approved.
- At least one short video or static workflow asset is approved.
- Report readiness checklist is exportable.
- UTM links are tested.
- Daily log is ready.
- Outreach list is prepared.
- No paid spend is enabled without explicit approval.
