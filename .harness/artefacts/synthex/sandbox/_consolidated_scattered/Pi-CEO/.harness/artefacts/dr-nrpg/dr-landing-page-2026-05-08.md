## THE PROBLEM

### You built a good business. Now it's running you.

Most restoration operators don't have a management problem. They have a systems problem. And until that's solved, growth just means more pressure on the person at the top.

---

**Everything depends on you.**

Your techs call you. Your office calls you. Jobs move when you move them. The business doesn't run when you're not watching — it waits.

**You're operating blind.**

You know roughly what's happening. But you can't see all jobs in one place, you can't see which are at risk, and you can't see what your team actually completed last week without asking.

**You're always reacting.**

Documentation gets done at the end. Compliance is assembled in a rush. Decisions are made on gut feel because there's no data — just memory and experience holding everything together.

---

This is not a character flaw. It's a systems gap. And it's the same gap every restoration business hits when it grows past the point where one person can hold it all.

DR closes that gap.

## THE SOLUTION

### One platform. Complete operational control.

---

### Operations Platform

Job management built for restoration workflows — not adapted from construction software and forced to fit.

From first notice of loss to close-out pack, every job lives in DR. Assign techs, track progress, log site activity, manage documentation, and monitor SLA status in real time. Your business runs whether you're on-site or not.

---

### Practitioner Network

DR connects directly to NRPG — the National Restoration Practitioners Group — the ANZ restoration community for independent operators.

Platform users get access to peer benchmarking, shared knowledge, and the collective intelligence of restoration businesses who have solved the same problems. Technology and community reinforce each other. Your network grows as your systems grow.

---

### Compliance Integration

Stop assembling close-out packs from scattered files at the end of a job.

DR builds compliance into the workflow. Moisture logs, photo documentation, scope sign-off, and completion records are generated from the job itself — not reconstructed from memory. Every job is audit-ready. Every claim is defensible.

## HOW IT WORKS

### Three steps from chaos to operational control

**1. Map your business into DR**
Bring your existing jobs, team structure, and workflows into the platform. DR is configured for restoration from day one — no heavy implementation, no consultants.

**2. Run your first jobs on the platform**
Your team works from DR. Job status is live. Documentation happens in the field. You stop being the communication layer between your techs and your office.

**3. Scale with visibility**
With your operations running on data instead of memory, you can see what's working, fix what isn't, and grow without creating new chaos. The platform scales with you.

## INTEGRATIONS

### The complete ANZ restoration business ecosystem

DR is the operational core of a three-part stack built for ANZ restoration businesses.

---

**NRPG — National Restoration Practitioners Group**
The ANZ practitioner community. DR platform users get access to NRPG membership — peer groups, education, the Structure → Systems → Scale framework, and the independence movement redefining how ANZ restorers operate.

**RestoreAssist**
Client-facing and insurer-interface tooling that connects to your DR job records. Property owner communication, scope approval, and insurance interactions handled through a system — not your personal phone.

**Synthex**
Reporting, data infrastructure, and business intelligence layer. Pull performance data from DR into Synthex for benchmarking, trend analysis, and the business reporting that mature operators need.
