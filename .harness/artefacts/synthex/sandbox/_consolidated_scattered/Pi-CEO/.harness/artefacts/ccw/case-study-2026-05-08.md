# How CCW Achieved a 34% Email Open Rate and Cut Operational Setup Time by 70%

**Client:** Carpet Cleaners Warehouse (CCW)
**Sector:** Trade Equipment — Wholesale & Distribution
**Platform:** Shopify E-Commerce + Unite-Group CRM-ERP + Synthex Marketing Automation

## About CCW

Carpet Cleaners Warehouse is a UK-based trade wholesaler and distributor of professional carpet cleaning equipment, supplying contractors, cleaning companies, and trade buyers through their Shopify-powered e-commerce platform. Their business runs on speed and reliability — trade prices, same-day dispatch, and a customer base that expects expert service without the wait.

---

## The Challenge

Before engaging Unite-Group, CCW was scaling faster than their operational infrastructure could support. Three problems were compounding:

**1. Fragmented Customer and Order Data**
CCW's customer records, order history, and trade account information were spread across disconnected systems — their Shopify store, spreadsheets, and a legacy quoting tool. Sales staff were manually cross-referencing data to handle trade queries, creating delays and errors on high-volume days. There was no single source of truth.

**2. Slow, Manual Campaign Execution**
Marketing campaigns required significant manual setup — exporting lists, cleaning data, importing into their email platform, and building segments by hand. A single campaign could take the better part of a day to go live. With a trade audience that responds to time-sensitive offers and stock alerts, that lag was costing revenue.

**3. Manual Quoting and Account Management**
Trade customers — contractors and cleaning businesses placing regular bulk orders — expected fast, accurate quotes. CCW's team was generating quotes manually, with no automation around pricing tiers, account history, or follow-up. Response times were inconsistent, and staff capacity was a constant bottleneck.

---

## The Solution

Unite-Group designed and built an integrated operational stack centred on a custom CRM-ERP system (`ccw-crm`) and a direct integration with Synthex, CCW's marketing automation platform. The goal: unify data, automate the repetitive, and give CCW's team leverage.

**Custom CRM-ERP Implementation**
The `ccw-crm` system was built to be the operational core — a single source of truth connecting CCW's Shopify store data, trade account records, order history, pricing tiers, and quoting workflows. Trade account information that previously lived across five different places now lives in one. Staff can pull up a full customer picture — purchase history, account tier, outstanding quotes, communications — in seconds.

**Shopify Integration**
Unite-Group connected `ccw-crm` directly to CCW's Shopify store, meaning customer data, order data, and inventory signals flow in real time. New trade account sign-ups are automatically provisioned, order history is immediately available to the sales team, and pricing tier logic is applied consistently without manual intervention.

**Synthex Marketing Automation Integration**
The CRM-ERP feeds clean, structured customer segments directly into Synthex. Where CCW's team previously spent hours building campaign audiences manually, they can now move from segment definition to a live campaign in under two hours. Synthex handles the sequencing, personalisation, and delivery — with CCW's team focused on strategy, not data wrangling.

**SLA-Backed Support Infrastructure**
Unite-Group implemented a tiered support structure for CCW's ongoing operations: a 15-minute response SLA for warning-level issues and a 60-minute SLA for critical incidents. For a trade business where same-day dispatch is a core value proposition, operational uptime is non-negotiable.
