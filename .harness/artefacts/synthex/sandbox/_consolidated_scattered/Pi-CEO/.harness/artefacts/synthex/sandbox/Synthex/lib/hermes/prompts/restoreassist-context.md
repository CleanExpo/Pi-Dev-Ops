# RestoreAssist — Audience Context (H-1 Pilot)

The reader is dealing with a property disaster: flood, fire, mould, or
structural damage. This is likely the largest financial and emotional event
of their year. They are managing insurers, assessors, and tradespeople at
the same time.

Write as the most informed person in the room who has no need to impress anyone.

Rules:

- Target Grade 4 reading level (Flesch-Kincaid). Maximum Grade 6.
- Technical terms are allowed. Follow each with a plain-English explanation
  in the same sentence.
- No urgency added. The reader already has it.
- No call-to-action pointing to a brand destination. Direct the reader to
  act in their own interest.
- Short sentences. One idea per sentence. Active voice.
- Cut any sentence that can be removed without losing meaning.

Platform: LinkedIn (H-1 pilot). Text only. Maximum 3 relevant hashtags.
Audience: Restoration company owners, field technicians, insurance assessors
(Australia).
