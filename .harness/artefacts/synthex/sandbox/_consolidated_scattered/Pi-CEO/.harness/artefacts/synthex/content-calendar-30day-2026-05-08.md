**Brand:** Synthex | **Primary Platform:** LinkedIn
**Primary Audience:** Trade-sector SMB owners in ANZ (restoration, cleaning, construction)
**Secondary Audience:** Marketing agencies and operators
**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2026-05-08

## Voice Guide

Direct. No fluff. Short sentences. Data when available. No corporate jargon.
Speaks to people who are on the tools at 6am and checking their phone at 9pm.
References the "Structure → Systems → Scale" framework — build the foundation first, systemise it, then grow. Work for the client, not the 3rd party.
The reader respects honesty over polish.

## Week 1 (Days 1–7): Theme — "Why Social Media Is Broken for Trade Businesses"

**Day 1: Opinion/Thought Leadership**
**Hook:** "Every tradie I talk to has the same problem. They're paying $2,000/month to an agency and getting nothing back."
The post breaks down why traditional agency models fail trade businesses — generic content, no industry knowledge, no accountability for leads. Ends with: "You shouldn't need an agency to have a professional social presence. The system should work without you."

**Day 2: Industry Data**
**Hook:** "AI search now answers 40% of business-related queries without a click. If you're not in the answer, you don't exist."
Walks through the split between traditional Google and AI search (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews). Explains why posting once a week on Facebook isn't enough anymore. Ends with the practical implication: consistent, structured content now equals two types of visibility.

**Day 3: Engagement Question**
**Hook:** "Quick question for trade business owners: how many hours per week do you (or someone on your team) spend on social media?"
Asks the audience to reply with their number. Provides context — the industry average for SMBs attempting to manage social in-house is 6–10 hours/week. Invites comments on what that time is actually spent on. Low production, high signal.

**Day 4: Educational Carousel**
**Hook:** "The 3-step reason your social posts aren't generating leads (and what actually fixes it)."
Carousel walks through: Step 1 — Inconsistency (algorithms punish gaps), Step 2 — Platform-agnostic content (posting the same thing everywhere doesn't work), Step 3 — No AEO structure (content not readable by AI search). Ends with the fix: structure, consistency, dual-surface optimisation.

**Day 5: Tool Tip**
**Hook:** "One thing that will double the reach of every before/after photo you post."
Practical tip on how to geo-tag, keyword-load, and format before/after posts so they get found in both Google Images and AI search. No product pitch. Pure value. Mentions that this is exactly the structure Synthex builds automatically.

**Day 6: Opinion/Thought Leadership**
**Hook:** "The 'Structure → Systems → Scale' order matters. Most trade businesses try to scale before they have systems."
Applies the framework to social media specifically. Structure = consistent brand voice and posting schedule. Systems = content creation and scheduling that runs without manual input. Scale = growing reach and inbound leads. Explains why skipping step two causes burnout and abandoned marketing.

**Day 7: Direct CTA (Trial Offer)**
**Hook:** "Your social media should be working while you're on the job. Here's how to set that up this week."
Explains what Synthex does in plain terms — connects your platforms, learns your voice, builds and schedules content. References CCW: 70% setup time reduction. Ends with direct link to start a free trial. Single clear action.

## Week 3 (Days 15–21): Theme — "AI Search Is the New Word of Mouth"

**Day 15: Opinion/Thought Leadership**
**Hook:** "Word of mouth used to mean a neighbour recommending you. Now it means a ChatGPT answer recommending you. Has your business caught up?"
Makes the case that AI search is the new word of mouth — trusted, conversational, and based on aggregated signals rather than a single review. For trade businesses, this means consistent content is now a reputation infrastructure investment, not just a marketing activity.

**Day 16: Educational Carousel**
**Hook:** "What is AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation) and why does every trade business need it in 2026?"
Carousel defines AEO without jargon: answering the exact questions customers ask in AI search tools. Walks through three examples: a restoration company, a cleaning business, a builder — and shows what AEO-structured content looks like versus generic posts. Ends with a simple framework: every post should answer a question someone would ask Google or ChatGPT.

**Day 17: Tool Tip**
**Hook:** "Copy this post structure to make your content show up in AI search answers."
Practical template: [Problem statement] + [Specific answer with entity-rich detail] + [Proof or credential] + [Local/service context] + [Clear CTA]. Gives an example using a fictional restoration company. Shows exactly how to format a LinkedIn post for AEO. Pure value.

**Day 18: Behind-the-Scenes**
**Hook:** "We tested the same content — one written for traditional SEO, one written for AEO — on the same brand. The results were different. Here's what we found."

**Day 20: Educational Carousel**

Urgency-led but not pushy. Explains that AI search presence is built over 30–90 days of consistent, structured content. The best time to start was 90 days ago. The second-best time is now. Links to free trial. References 1,000+ brands already on the platform.

- **Recommended posting time (ANZ):** 7:00–8:30am or 12:00–1:00pm AEST (highest SMB LinkedIn activity)
- **Frequency:** Daily for the 30-day sprint, then 4–5x/week sustained
- **Hashtag approach:** 3–5 targeted tags per post. Core set: #TradeBusinessANZ #RestorationIndustry #SocialMediaForTradies #AIMarketing #SynthexSocial
- **Engagement protocol:** Respond to every comment within 4 hours on Days 1–7 (seeding the algorithm)
- **Repurposing:** Carousel content from Days 4, 11, 16, 20, 22 can be repurposed as Instagram static posts and Facebook album posts within the same Synthex workflow
