## The AEO-Specific Angle: Ranking in AI Search AND Traditional Search Simultaneously

### What Changed
In 2024–2026, search bifurcated. Traditional Google search still matters — local SEO, long-tail queries, image search. But AI Overviews (Google's own AI summaries), ChatGPT search, Perplexity, and Claude.ai now answer millions of queries directly — without a click.

The result: a business can rank #1 on Google and still be invisible to anyone searching via AI. Two entirely separate optimisation problems.

Most businesses are still optimising only for one. Most tools are built for only one.

### How Synthex Solves Both
Synthex-generated content is structured with dual-optimisation in mind:

**For traditional search (Google):**
- Consistent posting signals to Google that the brand is active
- Location-tagged, keyword-rich captions for local SEO lift
- Platform content indexes in Google Search (LinkedIn posts, YouTube descriptions, Reddit threads)

**For AI search (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews):**
- Content written in Q&A and conversational formats that AI search engines extract directly
- Entity-dense language (named services, locations, specific outcomes) that large language models parse and cite
- Consistent brand narrative across platforms — LLMs synthesise reputation from aggregate signals, not single pages
- Topic authority built through consistent, vertical-specific posting (a cleaning company posting about IICRC standards and water damage protocols becomes the cited authority)

**The combined effect:** A Synthex-managed brand appears both in traditional search results AND in AI-generated answers. When someone Googles "restoration company Melbourne," they find the website. When they ask ChatGPT "who's the best restoration company in Melbourne," the AI has enough brand signal to cite the name.

No other social media tool in the market is building content with this dual-surface logic. This is Synthex's defensible positioning for the next 3–5 years.
