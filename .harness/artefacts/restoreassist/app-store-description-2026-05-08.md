• IICRC-Compliant Drying Logs — automatic log formatting that meets WRT and CDS documentation standards
• Moisture Mapping — record and visualise readings across affected areas, room by room
• Photo Capture with Timestamps — every photo geo-tagged and timestamped at the moment it's taken, not when you get around to it
• Professional Job Reports — send a PDF report that looks like it came from a company three times your size
• Scope Documentation — capture what was agreed, what was found, and what was completed. No ambiguity
• Dispute-Ready Records — when an adjuster pushes back, you have the file ready. Date, time, moisture level, photo, signature
• Cloud Sync — your jobs are backed up, accessible, and shareable from anywhere
