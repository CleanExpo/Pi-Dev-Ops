**Subject line:** How to get the most out of your CCW trade account

**Preview text:** Bulk pricing, fast reorders, and a few things most customers miss at the start.

---

**Body:**

Hi [First Name],

Three days in — hope you've had a chance to explore the account. We wanted to share a few things that our most active trade customers use every day.

**Bulk pricing tiers are automatic.**
Order more, pay less — it's that simple. Your pricing adjusts automatically when you hit volume thresholds. No haggling, no calling us. If you're placing regular orders for a cleaning business or a team, it's worth knowing exactly where those thresholds sit. [See the pricing guide here.]

**Fast reorder from your dashboard.**
Every previous order is saved. If you're restocking the same products — solution, pads, machine parts — you can reorder in two clicks directly from your order history. No searching, no re-adding to cart.

**Same-day dispatch cutoff is 2pm.**
Orders placed before 2pm Monday–Friday ship the same day. If you're planning a job and need kit by tomorrow, that's your window. Set a reminder if it helps — it's made a big difference for our contractors.

**Out-of-stock alerts.**
If something you need is temporarily out, you can set a stock alert directly on the product page. We'll email you the moment it's available — so you're not checking back manually.

If there's anything you can't find or a product you need that's not listed, email us directly. We source to order for regular trade accounts.
