## 8 AEO Questions (Answer Engine Optimisation)

These target featured snippets, SGE (Search Generative Experience), and voice search. Write concise 40–60 word direct-answer paragraphs for each, then expand into full articles.

1. **What carpet cleaning equipment do professional cleaners use?**
   - Intent: New-to-trade buyers researching their first kit purchase
   - Target format: Bulleted list (machine types + use cases)

2. **What is the best commercial carpet extractor in Australia?**
   - Intent: Product comparison, high purchase intent
   - Target format: Table (model vs. application vs. price tier)

3. **Where can I buy cleaning chemicals wholesale in Australia?**
   - Intent: B2B buyers looking for a distributor
   - Target format: Direct answer paragraph + CCW as solution

4. **How much does commercial carpet cleaning equipment cost?**
   - Intent: Budget research, mid-funnel
   - Target format: Price-range breakdown by category

5. **What is the difference between a carpet extractor and a steam cleaner?**
   - Intent: Education, early funnel
   - Target format: Definition comparison table

6. **Do I need a licence to start a carpet cleaning business in Australia?**
   - Intent: Start-up trade buyers (high lifetime value)
   - Target format: State-by-state summary + CTA to equipment guide

7. **What cleaning chemicals are safe for commercial carpet cleaning?**
   - Intent: Product safety / compliance research
   - Target format: Category list with use-case notes

8. **How often should commercial carpets be professionally cleaned?**
   - Intent: End-customer education; positions CCW as industry authority
   - Target format: Frequency guide by environment type

## Technical SEO Priorities

1. **Schema markup:** Product schema on all product pages (price, availability, SKU); FAQ schema on all guide pages for AEO questions
2. **Core Web Vitals:** Image optimisation critical — equipment photography is large-file-heavy
3. **Crawl structure:** Max 3 clicks from homepage to any product
4. **Canonical URLs:** Set per product variant (size/colour/configuration) to avoid thin-content penalties
5. **Hreflang:** AU primary; consider NZ variant pages once AU is established
6. **Page speed:** Same-day dispatch promise only credible if site loads fast — 3s max mobile LCP
