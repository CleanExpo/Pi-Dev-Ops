## Production Notes

- **Instagram Reels priority days:** 4 (dispatch), 8 (warehouse), 23 (order-to-dispatch), 29 (top sellers). Short-form video outperforms static for reach on IG.
- **LinkedIn post length:** 150–250 words. No listicles padded to 10 items. Trade buyers scroll fast.
- **Photography brief:** Natural light, warehouse/job-site setting preferred over studio. Show equipment in use, not on a white background. Workers in CCW-branded gear if available.
- **Instagram link strategy:** All product/shop CTAs → linktree or bio link. Track clicks by UTM per post type.
- **Timing:** Instagram — 7–9am or 6–8pm AEST. LinkedIn — Tuesday–Thursday 8–10am AEST for maximum organic reach.
- **Cross-posting rule:** Case study content can cross from LinkedIn to Instagram as condensed carousels. Trade tips work both directions. Brand/CTA posts should be platform-native (don't copy-paste).
