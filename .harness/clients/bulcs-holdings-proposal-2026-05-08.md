## The AEO/GEO Opportunity

Answer Engine Optimisation (AEO) and Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO) are the emerging discipline of making your content the source that AI assistants (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude) cite when professionals ask questions.

**The current situation in ANZ restoration/IAQ:** No competitor has structured their content for AI retrieval. Restore Solutions has better YouTube volume. Instrument Choice has more content. But neither has FAQ schema, structured data, or AEO-intentional content.

**What this means for Bulcs Holdings:** The first brand to publish authoritative, well-structured answers to the questions restoration contractors and facility managers are asking AI assistants will own that answer space for months, possibly years.

Target questions include:
- "What moisture meter do I need for IICRC S500 compliance in Australia?"
- "When should I use desiccant vs LGR dehumidification?"
- "What are the NCC 2022 IAQ requirements for commercial buildings in Australia?"
- "How do I document moisture readings for an insurance claim in Australia?"

These questions are being asked right now. Ivi's expertise is the correct answer. The gap is structured content that surfaces that expertise in the format AI engines consume.

Every piece of content we produce will include FAQ schema, structured headers, and AEO-optimised formatting as standard.

## ROI Case: MME E-Commerce Revenue Impact

Moisture Meter Experts is the clearest revenue-attribution opportunity in the portfolio. It's e-commerce — traffic converts to orders.

**Assumptions (conservative):**
- Current monthly organic visitors to moisturemeterexperts.com.au: estimated 1,000–2,500 (no live data yet; to be confirmed in Month 1 audit)
- Average order value: $400–$800 AUD (professional-grade moisture meters, multifunction kits)
- Conversion rate: 2% (industry standard for B2B e-commerce)

**Baseline monthly revenue from organic (midpoint):** 1,750 visitors × 2% = 35 orders × $600 = $21,000/month

**If organic traffic doubles in 90 days** (achievable with 5 optimised pages targeting commercial-intent keywords):
- 3,500 visitors × 2% = 70 orders × $600 = $42,000/month
- **Incremental revenue: $21,000/month**
- **Annual incremental revenue: $252,000 AUD**

At $2,000/month (Growth tier), the annual agency cost is $24,000. Against $252,000 in incremental organic revenue, that is a 10.5x ROI — and this does not include lead generation for AeroAir or IAQ Ventilation, which operate on much larger B2B deal sizes.

**Note:** These projections depend on the Month 1 baseline audit. If current traffic is lower than estimated, the absolute dollar figures adjust — but the percentage impact (doubling traffic via content) is consistent with comparable B2B e-commerce interventions.
