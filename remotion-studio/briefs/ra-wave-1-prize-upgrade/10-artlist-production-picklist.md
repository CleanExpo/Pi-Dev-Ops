## Asset Rules

- Use only assets licensed for commercial advertising/social use under the active Artlist account.
- Download license proof before editing with an asset.
- Avoid recognisable faces unless the license and release coverage are explicit.
- Avoid footage with visible third-party logos, addresses, licence plates, claim forms, insurer branding, or customer data.
- Prefer texture, atmosphere, hands, equipment, site detail, and abstract production plates over full narrative stock scenes.
