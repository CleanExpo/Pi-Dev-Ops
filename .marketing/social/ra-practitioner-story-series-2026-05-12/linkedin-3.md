Desk adjuster wrote "Category 2." Tech had S500 on the timestamp.

Sewage backup, ensuite, Sydney lower north shore. Initial scope came through as Cat-2 — clean-water protocols, no PPE escalation, no full content discard.

On arrival the tech documented the source: a still-running overflow from the sanitary stack. Photo, GPS, IICRC S500 classification note — all recorded inside the job file at 09:14.

Reclassification request was refused — not by argument, by the record. PPE escalation approved. Content discard approved. Antimicrobial application billed and paid.

Documentation is not paperwork. It is the difference between a $4,000 job and a $22,000 job.

How many Cat-3 losses started life as Cat-2 on someone's desk this year?
