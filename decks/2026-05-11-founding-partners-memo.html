02 · The Problem
60-second view · 5–50 FTE ANZ operator

Three frustrations run the day for a restoration or specialty-cleaning firm.

01
Pain · Dispatch

Insurer dispatch routes leads at random.

A panel firm down the road does a hack mould job for IAG; the carrier stops taking the good firm's calls for six months. No accountability. No shared certification floor.

02
Pain · Cert ladder

No ANZ cert ladder insurers or regulators actually recognise.

Anyone slaps "IICRC certified" on the van. The IICRC sits in Las Vegas with no skin in the local game. The firm cannot charge a premium when the cowboy down the street claims the same credential.

03
Pain · Marketing + tech

Marketing, branding, and tech burn the owner's nights.

Three agencies in five years. One website. Two ghostings after invoice two. The bookkeeper is also the website manager. The foreman is also the social-media intern at 9pm Tuesday.

07 · The Ask
Four commitments · all explicit · none buried

Founding Partner status is a lifetime pricing instrument and a directional advisory relationship.

01

Founding Membership commit

AU$249/mo · lifetime rate-lock · cap 100 firms total. Price never rises while the membership is unbroken. Once the 100-firm cap fills, the Founder tier closes permanently.

02

Quarterly advisory call

60 minutes, four times a year. Optional but valued. Agenda: Pi-CEO board's strategic questions for the quarter — pricing tests, channel mix, advocacy priorities, next Best Practice Brief.

03

Co-marketing rights

One LinkedIn post per quarter, mutual amplification. The association amplifies the founding partner's firm; the founding partner amplifies the association's milestone content. Editorial control stays on both sides.

04

Standards Committee seat — first five of ten Founders

Optional. Five seats reserved for the first five Founders signed. Unpaid, time-bound — one half-day per quarter. A recognition mechanism, not a workload.

× What this ask does NOT include
  • No upfront equity The association will not take, request, or be offered shares in the founding partner's firm.
  • No exclusivity Existing memberships, insurer panels, and supplier relationships remain unchanged.
  • No non-compete Founding partners are free to run any adjacent business, association, or trade group.
